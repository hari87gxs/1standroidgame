# Privacy Policy for Athreya's Sums - Math Workout Game

**Last updated: November 13, 2025**

## Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Athreya's Sums ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

## Information We Collect

**We do NOT collect any personal information from our users.**

Specifically, our app does not collect:
- Names, email addresses, or contact information
- Location data or device identifiers
- User accounts or login credentials
- Payment information
- Photos, camera, or microphone access
- Any personal data whatsoever

## How Our App Works

**Local Storage Only:**
- All game progress, scores, and settings are stored locally on your device only
- No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties
- Your math workout progress remains completely private on your device

**No User Accounts:**
- No registration or sign-up required
- No usernames, passwords, or personal profiles
- Play immediately without providing any information

## Data Security

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no personal information at risk. All game data remains securely on your device under your control.

## Children's Privacy

Our app is designed to be safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or users of any age. The app is purely educational and entertainment-focused with no social features or data collection.

## Third-Party Services

Our app does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks that would collect user data.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Any updates will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

## Data Retention

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no personal information to retain or delete. All app data remains on your device and can be removed by uninstalling the app.

## Your Rights

You have complete control over any data related to our app:
- All game data is stored locally on your device
- You can clear app data through your device settings
- You can uninstall the app at any time to remove all associated data
