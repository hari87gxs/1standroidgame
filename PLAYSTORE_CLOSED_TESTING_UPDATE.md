# Updating Play Store Description During Closed Testing

## Important: For Apps in Closed Testing

When your app is in **closed testing** (internal testing or closed track), the Store Presence section has **limited availability** because your app isn't publicly visible yet. Here's what you need to know:

---

## Option 1: Prepare Store Listing for Future (Recommended)

### Step-by-Step:

1. **Access Play Console**
   - Go to https://play.google.com/console
   - Sign in with your developer account
   - Select "Athreya's Math Workout"

2. **Check if Main Store Listing is Available**
   - In the left sidebar, expand **"Grow"** or look for **"Store presence"**
   - Click on **"Main store listing"**
   
3. **If "Main store listing" is available:**
   - ‚úÖ You can update the description now!
   - Follow the normal update process (see below)
   - Changes will be saved but won't be visible until you publish to production
   
4. **If "Main store listing" is grayed out or not visible:**
   - ‚ö†Ô∏è You need to set up your store listing first
   - This is required before promoting to production anyway

---

## Option 2: Set Up Store Listing (If Not Done Yet)

If you can't access Main store listing, you need to complete the initial setup:

### Step 1: Navigate to Dashboard
1. In Play Console, select your app
2. Look at the left sidebar menu
3. Find **"Grow"** section or **"Store presence"**

### Step 2: Complete Required Store Information

You'll need to fill out these sections (in order):

#### A. Main Store Listing
1. Click on **"Main store listing"**
2. Fill in required fields:
   - **App name** (50 characters max)
   - **Short description** (80 characters max)
   - **Full description** (4000 characters max)
   - **App icon** (512 x 512 PNG)
   - **Feature graphic** (1024 x 500 JPG or PNG)
   - **Screenshots** (minimum 2, recommended 8)
     - Phone: 1080 x 1920 or 720 x 1280
     - Tablet (optional): 1920 x 1200 or 2560 x 1600

3. **Application type and category**
   - Type: Application
   - Category: Educational or Puzzle

4. **Contact details**
   - Email address (for user support)
   - Website (optional)
   - Phone number (optional)
   - Privacy policy URL (optional but recommended)

#### B. Upload Assets
- **App icon**: 512 x 512 PNG with transparency
- **Feature graphic**: 1024 x 500 JPG/PNG (banner at top of listing)
- **Screenshots**: At least 2 phone screenshots

#### C. Save Your Work
- Click **"Save"** at the bottom
- Don't worry, this won't publish your app
- It just prepares the store listing

## Option 3: Update Description After Initial Setup

Once the store listing is set up, here's how to update it:

### Navigate to Main Store Listing:
```
Play Console ‚Üí Your App ‚Üí Grow ‚Üí Store presence ‚Üí Main store listing
```

### Update the Description:

1. **Short Description** (80 chars max):


2. **Full Description** (4000 chars max):
   - Copy from `PLAYSTORE_DESCRIPTION.md`
   - Paste into the Full description field
   - Format with bullets and emojis

3. **Click "Save"**

## How to Update from Closed Testing to Production

When you're ready to make your app public (with the new description):

### Step 1: Ensure Store Listing is Complete
- All required fields filled in Main store listing
- Description updated with new content
- Screenshots uploaded
- App icon and graphics added
- Content rating completed
- Pricing set (free or paid)

### Step 2: Promote to Production
1. Go to **"Release"** ‚Üí **"Production"**
2. Click **"Create new release"**
3. Upload your signed APK/AAB (release build)
4. Review release notes
5. Review store listing one more time
6. Click **"Review release"**
7. Click **"Start rollout to Production"**

### Step 3: App Review
- Google will review your app (1-7 days, typically 1-2 days)
- You'll receive email notifications about status
- Once approved, your app goes live with the new description!

## Important Notes for Closed Testing

### What Testers See:
- Testers in closed testing don't see the full Play Store listing
- They get access through a special testing link
- They see a simplified page with basic app info

### What You Should Prepare:
- Even though testers don't see it, prepare your full store listing now
- This saves time when you're ready to launch publicly
- All changes are saved in draft until you promote to production

### Timeline:
- **Store listing updates in closed testing:** Saved immediately, visible only in console
- **After promoting to production:** Store listing goes live after app review (1-7 days)

## Common Questions

### Q: Will updating the description affect my testers?
**A:** No, closed testers access the app through a special link and don't see the store listing changes.

### Q: Can I update the description while in closed testing?
**A:** Yes! You can (and should) prepare the store listing even during testing. Changes won't be public until you promote to production.

### Q: Do I need to submit a new version to update the description?
**A:** No, store listing changes (description, screenshots) don't require a new app version.

### Q: When will my description changes be visible?
**A:** Not until you promote your app to production and it's approved by Google.
